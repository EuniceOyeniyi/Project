Emotion based Voice Assitance

Key Features:

  • Plays music based emtional state predicted from voice

  • Automatically plays music base on time of the day
    • Morning
    • Afternoon
    • Evening

  • Personal control of music or video choice

Project Motivation

The project idea was triggered from my personal need as a first time mum.

It's a new phase of life filled with different emotional state, some days are overwhelming, exhausting, exciting, stressful and painful.

Why

  • Music can accommodate every kind of mood you can possibly have.
  • It also has the power to heal your mental and emotional state.
  • It's a convient way to entertain or get yourself relaxed without typing or using a remote control
